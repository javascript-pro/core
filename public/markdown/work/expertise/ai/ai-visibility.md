> As search evolves into AI-driven answers, visibility is no longer about ranking on page one — it’s about being the source that answer engines trust and cite. To win mentions and citations, your content must be:

- Authoritative – backed by expertise, reliable sources, and clear ownership.

- Structured – formatted for machines (clean schema, semantic headings, concise summaries).

- Contextual – answering real user questions with depth, not just keywords.

- Credible – consistent across channels, with strong signals of trust (links, reviews, engagement).

Answer engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google’s AI Overviews increasingly offer credible, high-clarity content. That means shifting from thinking in SEO keywords to AI discoverability: writing for algorithms and humans
