## Vanlife (noun)

- A lifestyle built around living or traveling in a converted van, often equipped with a bed, kitchen, and storage, sometimes a bathroom.

- The practice of turning a vehicle into both home and transport, usually for minimalism, freedom, or adventure.

- A cultural movement popularized through social media, showcasing sunsets, mountain roads, and cozy interiors lit by fairy lights.

- The art of quitting your lease, shoving your worldly possessions into a van, and convincing yourself that showering in rivers builds character.
