> Lamnidae family

Large shark with short, pointed snout with wide mouth equipped with big triangular teeth with saw-toothed edges. The first dorsal fin is tall and developed. The caudal is characteristically sickle-shaped with almost identical lobes. The colouration is dark grey on the back and white underneath. Predator on top of the food chain, it feeds mainly on tuna and small cetaceans. Reaches lengths of over 7 metres.
